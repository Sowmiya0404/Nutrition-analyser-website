Online Tools

Nutrition.gov's Online Tools collection offers videos, quizzes, calculators, and games to promote healthy eating and physical activity. Users can watch informative videos, take quizzes to test their knowledge, use calculators to plan meals and track progress, and engage with interactive games for learning. Expanded Food and Nutrition Education Program (EFNEP)

Federal Cooperative Extension programs play a crucial role in promoting community health and nutrition security through education initiatives. These programs, administered by the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), provide evidence-based information and resources to communities nationwide. They offer workshops, classes, and demonstrations on topics such as healthy eating, food safety, gardening, and physical activity.
